The Alfred W. Bowers
Laboratory of Anthropology

For Parents & Educators

Since the earliest days of the Bowers Lab, outreach has been essential part of our work.


Outreach comes in many forms, including hosting local school visits, discussing Idaho's history with undergraduate and graduate students at the University of Idaho, and bringing materials to local schools for show and tell. We develop and organize original content for these events, including workbooks, coloring pages, and games. Below is a sample of the outreach materials we have used for these events. Educators and parents are welcome to print them out and use them. We would greatly appreciate it if educators could let us know if they use our materials, and how they have worked in the classroom.

The below materials cannot be used for commercial reproduction.
