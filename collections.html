Collections & Holdings

The University of Idaho holds a diverse group of historic and prehistoric collections. Below is a list of our historic collections. If you wish to conduct research on them or gain access to them, you will need to work with the agency who owns the collection as well as the Bowers Laboratory. If you would like more information on them, please contact Dr. Leah Evans-Janke at leahe@uidaho.edu.


Highlighted links (items in the table that are white) will take you to additional information on the collection.
